About Us

Marksmen Consults is a real estate service provider and development solutions firm based in Lagos, Nigeria that was designed for the real estate marketplace of the future on the African continent. Marksmen consults advise corporate, institutional, and private clients who are seeking to acquire, lease, develop, or realize the value of residential and commercial property in Africa's key locations. Our expertise and relationships allow us to provide clients with the best deals in the market and ultimately provide investors with the best returns on their investments. We offer strategic and well-researched information on your dream real estate location while providing credible intel on viable investment opportunities on the continent. We have an incredible relationship with the leading developers and industrialists on the continent who have sound track records of delivering the best real estate projects and urban developments in the country and on the continent.
